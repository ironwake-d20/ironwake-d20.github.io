# The Church

The Church is the institutional religion of the default setting. It is a political institution with interests — not a source of objective moral truth.

## Doctrine

The Church's official doctrine on kai is deliberately ambiguous. Is it a divine gift? A cosmic accident? Heresy to ask? Different factions within the Church hold different positions, and the official line shifts with political convenience.

What is not ambiguous: class-bearers belong to the Church. They are registered at awakening, monitored throughout their lives, and deployed as the Church sees fit. A class-bearer who refuses service is a heretic.

## Enforcement arms

The Church maintains several enforcement arms:

- **Inquisitors** — Intelligence-based fighters (see [Inquisitor subclass](../character-creation/classes.md)). Investigators, interrogators, and hunters of unsanctioned class-bearers.
- **Templars** — Paladins and other faith-driven combatants who serve as the Church's military arm.

## Registration

All class-bearers are registered at awakening. Registration involves kai measurement, documentation, and assignment of a Church monitor. The registered bear a mark — physical or documentary — that identifies them to Church authorities.

Unregistered class-bearers are considered dangerous. The Church's position is that unregistered kai is a threat to the social order — and they are not entirely wrong.
