# The World

The default Ironwake setting is a medievalpunk world — medieval in its social structures and technology, but punctuated by the reality of kai. Kai-measuring instruments are a known technology. The Church maintains registries of class-bearers. Imbued artefacts circulate as contraband and currency among the desperate.

## Technology level

The baseline technology is late medieval: plate armour, crossbows, early firearms (setting-dependent), wind and water mills, the printing press. Kai-based technology — measurement instruments, artefact creation — exists alongside this but is controlled by the Church and its allied institutions.

## The social position of class-bearers

Class-bearers are treated by institutional religion as assets first and people second. This has mechanical consequences in social encounters, legal standing, and the attention of the Church's enforcement arms.

A registered class-bearer has legal protections but limited freedom. An unregistered class-bearer is a fugitive. A common person who acquires an imbued artefact is a criminal — or a hero, depending on who tells the story.
