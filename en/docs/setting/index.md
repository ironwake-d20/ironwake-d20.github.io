# Setting

The Ironwake rules ship with a default medievalpunk setting. This setting is the natural expression of the mechanics — a world where kai-measuring instruments exist alongside mail armour, where the Church registers class-bearers like nuclear assets, and where artificers craft single-use artefacts for desperate commoners.

## Reskinning

The setting is reskinnable. Subclass names, institutional names, and cosmological flavour can be changed to fit your table's world. The one hard requirement is a fiction that explains why some individuals have class potential (kai) and others do not.
