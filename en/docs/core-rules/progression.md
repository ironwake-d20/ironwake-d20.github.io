# Progression

Characters advance through experience, gaining levels that unlock new capabilities. However, your **kai tier** sets an absolute ceiling — no amount of experience can push you past what your innate capacity allows.

## Attribute Score Improvements (ASI)

At levels 4, 8, 12, 16, and 19, you may increase one attribute score by 2, or two attribute scores by 1 each. This cannot raise a score above 20 unless a specific feature allows it.

## Kai ceiling

Your kai tier determines your maximum possible level:

| Kai tier            | Max level |
| ------------------- | --------- |
| Spark (1,000–1,999) | ~5        |
| Ember (2,000–2,999) | ~8        |
| Flame (3,000–3,999) | ~13       |
| Forge (4,000–4,999) | ~17       |
| Blaze (5,000–5,999) | ~20       |
| Pyre+ (6,000+)      | ~20+      |

No player character begins below Forge tier.
