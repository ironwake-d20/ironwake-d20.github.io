# Core Rules

The core rules describe the five interlocking subsystems that define how Ironwake characters interact with the world.

## How the systems connect

- **Kai** sets the ceiling — your innate capacity determines how far you can progress.
- **Mana** is the spendable resource — drawn from your kai well, used for magic and subclass features.
- **Combat** is the primary physical resolution system — Vitality, Wounds, armour, and critical hits.
- **Magic** is the primary supernatural resolution system — eight schools, Fibonacci costs, and the sacrifice ring.
- **Pacts** are the escape hatch — single-use power at a known, permanent price.
- **Progression** is the reward loop — XP, levels, and what you gain as you advance.

These systems are designed to work together. Overdraw damages kai. Pacts can offer one last overdraw at permanent kai cost. A character who burns out is becoming less of what they were — knowingly, permanently.
