# Character Creation

Follow these steps in order to create your Ironwake character. Each step links to its detailed rules page.

## Step-by-step

1. **Choose your kai tier** — Your innate capacity sets your level ceiling. See [Kai & Mana](../core-rules/kai-and-mana.md).
2. **Generate attributes** — Determine your six attribute scores. See [Attributes](attributes.md).
3. **Choose your lineage** — Your cultural background and upbringing. See [Lineages](lineages.md).
4. **Choose your class** — Fighter, Healer, Spellcaster, or Rogue. See [Classes](classes.md).
5. **Assign skill points** — Distribute points based on your kai tier band. See [Skills](skills.md).
6. **Select feats** — Choose your starting feats. See [Feats](feats.md).
7. **Purchase starting equipment** — Gear up for adventure. See [Starting Equipment](starting-equipment.md).
8. **Calculate derived stats** — Vitality, Wounds, mana pool, and other computed values.
