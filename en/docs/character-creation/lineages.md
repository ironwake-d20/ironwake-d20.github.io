# Lineages

Ironwake is society-centric. Your lineage represents your cultural background, upbringing, and social station — not a biological subspecies.

## Design note

The default setting assumes the institutional religion as the main power, with a few major churches as the most influential organisations. Non-human lineages are setting-specific extensions and are not assumed by the core rules. If your table introduces them, they should come with a fiction that explains their relationship to kai.

### Example lineages

| Lineage          | Description                                                                                             |
| ---------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Church-raised    | Raised within the institutional religion. Familiar with doctrine, hierarchy, and the politics of faith. |
| Guild-born       | Born into a trade guild. Practical skills, contacts, and an understanding of commerce.                  |
| Frontier-settled | Grew up on the edges of civilisation. Self-reliant, hardy, and accustomed to hardship.                  |
| Urban-underclass | Survived the streets of a city. Streetwise, resourceful, and invisible to authority.                    |
| Nomadic          | Raised in a travelling community. Adaptable, perceptive, and unbound by borders.                        |
| Academic         | Educated in a formal institution. Learned, connected, and perhaps dangerously curious.                  |
| Military         | Trained from youth for service. Disciplined, tactical, and marked by the institution.                   |
