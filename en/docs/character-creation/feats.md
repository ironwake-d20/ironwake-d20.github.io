# Feats

Feats represent special capabilities, techniques, and talents that distinguish a character from others of the same class. They are chosen at character creation and at specific levels during progression.

## Feat slots

Characters gain feat slots at creation and at certain levels. The number of feats a character can have is limited by their level and kai tier.

### Magic feats

Abilities that enhance or modify spellcasting.

#### Free Casting I

**Prerequisite:** Spellcaster class, caster level 1+

Cantrips cost 0 mana.

#### Free Casting II

**Prerequisite:** Free Casting I, caster level 5+

Level 1 spells cost 0 mana. Once per day, you may cast a level 1 spell at level 2 effect for free.

#### Free Casting III

**Prerequisite:** Free Casting II, caster level 9+

Level 2 spells cost 0 mana. Once per day, you may cast a level 2 spell at level 3 effect for free.
