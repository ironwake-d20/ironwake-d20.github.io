# Equipment

Equipment covers everything a character can buy, carry, or craft — from weapons and armour to adventuring gear and imbued artefacts.

## Currency

The standard currency system uses three metals:

| Coin   | Symbol | Relative value |
| ------ | ------ | -------------- |
| Copper | cp     | 1              |
| Silver | sp     | 10 cp          |
| Gold   | gp     | 100 cp (10 sp) |

## Trade and barter

In the medievalpunk setting, coin is common in cities but barter dominates rural areas. The Church's economic influence means certain goods (kai-measuring instruments, imbued artefacts) are regulated or restricted.
