# Glossary

Alphabetical reference of all mechanical terms used in Ironwake.

## A

**Armour Tier** — Rating from 0 (unarmoured) to 4 (full plate) that determines how injury dice are rolled when a hit reaches Wounds. See [Armour & Shields](../equipment/armour-and-shields.md).

**ASI (Attribute Score Improvement)** — At certain levels, a character may increase one attribute score by 2 or two scores by 1. See [Progression](../core-rules/progression.md).

## B

**Base Die** — The damage die of a weapon, representing the force of the hit. Scales with weapon, not character level. See [Combat](../core-rules/combat.md).

## C

**Cantrip** — A minor spell that costs 1 mana (or 0 with Free Casting I). See [Magic](../core-rules/magic.md).

**Class** — A calling that requires sufficient kai to manifest. The four classes are Fighter, Healer, Spellcaster, and Rogue. See [Classes](../character-creation/classes.md).

**Critical Hit** — An attack that bypasses Vitality entirely and strikes Wounds directly. Requires meeting or exceeding the Critical Threshold. See [Combat](../core-rules/combat.md).

**Critical Threshold (CT)** — The number an attacker must meet or beat (in addition to hitting) to score a critical hit. `CT = 15 + Defender Armour Tier − Attacker Precision Bonus`. See [Combat](../core-rules/combat.md).

## F

**Free Casting** — A feat chain that reduces the mana cost of low-level spells to zero. See [Feats](../character-creation/feats.md).

## G

**Grievous Wound** — The most severe wound category (injury die 5–6). Requires a surgeon and extended recovery. See [Combat](../core-rules/combat.md).

## I

**Imbued Artefact** — An object created by a spellcaster that stores a spell effect, usable by non-class-bearers. See [Imbued Artefacts](../equipment/imbued-artefacts.md).

**Injury Die** — A d6 rolled when a hit reaches Wounds, determining wound severity (1–2 Minor, 3–4 Serious, 5–6 Grievous). See [Combat](../core-rules/combat.md).

## K

**Kai** — The innate capacity for class-level ability. Fixed at awakening; cannot be trained. Measured in points and grouped into tiers. See [Kai & Mana](../core-rules/kai-and-mana.md).

## M

**Mana** — The spendable resource for kai-driven abilities. Pool size = (10 × caster level) + (INT modifier × caster level). See [Kai & Mana](../core-rules/kai-and-mana.md).

**Minor Wound** — The least severe wound category (injury die 1–2). Requires rest and basic treatment. See [Combat](../core-rules/combat.md).

## O

**Overdraw** — Spending mana past zero. Causes permanent kai degradation. See [Kai & Mana](../core-rules/kai-and-mana.md).

## P

**Pact** — A single-use, supernaturally binding agreement between a person and an entity drawn to their despair. See [Pacts](../core-rules/pacts.md).

**Precision** — A weapon property that reduces the Critical Threshold, representing the ability to bypass armour. Weapon-gated. See [Combat](../core-rules/combat.md).

## S

**School (of magic)** — One of eight domains of magic: Abjuration, Conjuration, Divination, Enchantment, Evocation, Illusion, Necromancy, Transmutation. See [Magic](../core-rules/magic.md).

**Serious Wound** — A moderate wound category (injury die 3–4). Requires a physician and time. See [Combat](../core-rules/combat.md).

**Specialist** — A Spellcaster subclass that sacrifices access to schools of magic for deeper power in one chosen school. See [Magic](../core-rules/magic.md).

**Subclass** — A specialisation within a class, acquired during levels 4–13. See [Classes](../character-creation/classes.md).

## V

**Vitality** — Stamina, adrenaline, near-misses, and armour absorption. Depletes freely in combat; recovers relatively quickly. See [Combat](../core-rules/combat.md).

## W

**Wounds** — Actual anatomical damage. A fixed, low number (CON ÷ 4, minimum 1). Hard to recover; Serious and Grievous wounds require a surgeon. See [Combat](../core-rules/combat.md).
