# Character Sheet

The character sheet is the primary reference for your character during play. It records everything from attributes and derived stats to equipment and notes.

## Sections of the character sheet

### Identity

- Character name
- Player name
- Lineage (cultural background)
- Class and subclass(es)
- Kai tier and exact kai value
- Level and XP

### Attributes

- Strength, Dexterity, Constitution, Intelligence, Wisdom, Charisma
- Score and modifier for each
- Space to note ASIs taken

### Derived stats

- **Vitality maximum** — Derived from Constitution and class
- **Current Vitality** — Tracks depletion during combat
- **Wounds maximum** — Constitution ÷ 4 (minimum 1)
- **Current Wounds** — Tracks anatomical damage
- **Mana pool maximum** — (10 × caster level) + (INT modifier × caster level)
- **Current mana** — Tracks expenditure
- **Overdraw events** — Tracks permanent kai degradation

### Combat

- Armour tier and type
- Shield (if equipped)
- Weapon entries (name, base die, Precision, properties)
- Critical Threshold (calculated)

### Skills

- Skill list with proficiency marks and total bonuses

### Feats

- Feat name, source, and brief effect summary

### Equipment

- Carried items, weight/encumbrance
- Currency (cp, sp, gp)
- Imbued artefacts (if any)

### Wounds & conditions

- Active wound locations and severities
- Active conditions and their effects

### Notes

- Pacts made (offering, price, binding)
- Contacts, factions, reputation
- Campaign notes
