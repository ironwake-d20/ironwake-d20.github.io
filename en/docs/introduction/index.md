# Introduction

This section covers the fundamentals of playing Ironwake: how the core dice mechanic works, what makes up a character, and what you need to get started.

## What you need to play

- This rulebook
- A set of polyhedral dice (d4, d6, d8, d10, d12, d20)
- A character sheet (see [Appendices](../appendices/character-sheet.md))
- Pencil and paper
- 2–6 players, one of whom is the Game Master (GM)

## How this book is organised

1. **Introduction** — You are here. Core concepts and how to play.
2. **Character Creation** — Build your character step by step.
3. **Core Rules** — Kai, energy, classes, combat, magic, pacts, and progression.
4. **Adventuring** — Rest, travel, conditions, and downtime.
5. **Spells** — The full spell lists by school.
6. **Equipment** — Weapons, armour, gear, and imbued artefacts.
7. **Setting** — The default medievalpunk world and its cosmology.
8. **Appendices** — Glossary, quick reference, and character sheet.
