# Downtime

Between adventures, characters can pursue long-term activities. Downtime is measured in days, weeks, or months.

## Downtime activities

### Crafting

Create weapons, armour, or other equipment. Requires tools, materials, and time. See [Equipment](../equipment/index.md) for item details.

### Training

Learn a new skill, language, or tool proficiency. Requires a trainer, time, and gold.

### Research

Uncover lore, identify magic items, or investigate mysteries. Requires access to a library, sage, or other source of knowledge.

### Carousing

Build contacts, gather rumours, and establish a reputation. Requires gold and time spent in social settings.

### Recuperation

Recover from Grievous wounds under a surgeon's care. Requires gold, time, and a skilled physician.

### Imbuing

Spellcasters can create imbued artefacts during downtime. See [Imbued Artefacts](../equipment/imbued-artefacts.md) for rules.
