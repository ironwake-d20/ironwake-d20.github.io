# Adventuring

The adventuring section covers what happens between combats and spellcasting — the rhythm of travel, rest, and the conditions that affect characters in the field.

## The adventuring day

An adventuring day is the cycle of exploration, encounter, and rest. Time passes at different scales depending on the action:

| Scale  | Unit       | Used for                      |
| ------ | ---------- | ----------------------------- |
| Round  | 6 seconds  | Combat, chase scenes          |
| Minute | 10 rounds  | Exploration, searching a room |
| Hour   | 60 minutes | Short rests, overland travel  |
| Day    | 24 hours   | Long rests, downtime          |

## The scene

The **scene** is the narrative unit of play. A scene might be a combat encounter, a tense negotiation, or the exploration of a ruin. The GM frames scenes and transitions between them. Some mechanics (ongoing Vitality loss from Serious wounds, for example) operate at the scene level.
