# Rest & Recovery

Recovery in Ironwake is not instant. Vitality returns relatively quickly, but Wounds and mana require time — and in some cases, skilled intervention.

## Short rest

A short rest is a period of downtime lasting at least 1 hour. During a short rest:

- **Vitality:** Recover a portion of your maximum Vitality.
- **Wounds:** No recovery.
- **Mana:** No recovery.

## Long rest

A long rest is a period of extended downtime lasting at least 8 hours, during which a character sleeps or performs light activity. During a long rest:

- **Vitality:** Full recovery.
- **Wounds:** Minor wounds may recover with basic treatment. Serious and Grievous wounds do not recover.
- **Mana:** Recovery rate scales with kai tier. A Blaze-tier character recovers meaningfully overnight. A Forge-tier character may need 2–3 days to feel full after serious expenditure.

## Wound treatment

| Severity | Treatment required                           | Recovery time                          |
| -------- | -------------------------------------------- | -------------------------------------- |
| Minor    | Rest + basic treatment (bandaging, cleaning) | Days                                   |
| Serious  | Physician + time                             | Weeks                                  |
| Grievous | Surgeon + extended recovery                  | Months; may leave permanent impairment |

Magic cannot heal Serious or Grievous wounds. They sit outside the reach of spells — a surgeon is required.

## Mana recovery

Mana recovers with **time only**. There are no shortcuts in the base rules — no meditation, no potions, no ley line tapping. A wounded character on forced march still recovers, just more slowly.
