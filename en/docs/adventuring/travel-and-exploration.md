# Travel & Exploration

## Overland travel

Characters travelling overland move at a pace determined by their speed and the terrain.

### Travel pace

| Pace   | Effect                                |
| ------ | ------------------------------------- |
| Slow   | Stealth possible; better perception   |
| Normal | Standard pace                         |
| Fast   | Penalty to perception; cannot stealth |

### Navigation

When travelling through unfamiliar territory, the navigator makes a Wisdom (Survival) check against a TN set by the terrain. Failure means the party becomes lost.

### Foraging and tracking

Characters can forage for food and water or track creatures through the wilderness. These use Wisdom (Survival) checks.
